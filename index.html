Where Young Minds
Shape Political Discourse

Civitas Politics is an independent, youth-led platform based in Uzbekistan, dedicated to enhancing political literacy, fostering critical thinking, and giving a powerful voice to the younger generation. Founded in 2025 by high-schooler Biloliddin Boymurotov, it initially started as a simple, passionate school political club. Today, it has rapidly grown and proudly operates as an official member of the prestigious Civitas Global network.
